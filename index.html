HackSoc Notts Github Page

Welcome to the HackSoc Notts Github Page!

HackSoc Notts is a student society at the University Of Nottingham. You can find out more about us by visiting our website.

The projects on this account contain, or soon will contain, various projects we hope to work on throughout the year at our hackathons. Members are most welcome to work on what they want, but we have these suggestions as well.

If you have an idea for a project, don't hesitate to post to one of our social networks accounts (listed below), or even send an email to mail@hacksocnotts.co.uk.
